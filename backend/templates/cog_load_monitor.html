Facial Action Coding System
(FACS)

Our engine uses a lightweight Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to map 68 facial landmarks in real-time. By analyzing the micro-movements of your eyebrows, eyelids, and mouth corners, we calculate an aggregate stress score.

The AI looks for specific "Action Units" (AUs) typically associated with elevated cortisol and autonomic nervous system arousal, such as brow lowering (AU4) and lip tightening (AU23).
