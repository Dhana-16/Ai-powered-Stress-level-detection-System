Understanding Your Metrics

The NeuroCalm AI analysis is based on a composite of 20 distinct psychometric variables. These include academic performance pressure, sleep hygiene, social integration, and physiological symptom reporting.

High stress levels in students are often correlated with sleep deprivation and exam anxiety cycles. Our algorithm compares your input against a dataset of over 10,000 students to generate your percentile stress score.

If your score exceeds 75, immediate intervention via counseling or mindfulness techniques is strongly recommended. Scores between 40-75 indicate moderate stress, manageable through lifestyle adjustments such as increased physical activity and better time management.
