System FAQs

How does the AI analyze stress? +

Our AI uses rPPG (Remote Photoplethysmography) to detect subtle color changes in your skin caused by blood flow, combined with facial micro-expression analysis.

Is my biometric data secure? +

Absolutely. All processing is done locally in your browser via WebAssembly. No raw video or facial data leaves your device.

Can I integrate with my fitness tracker? +

Yes, our Synchronizer plan offers API access to sync with Apple Health, Google Fit, and Garmin devices.
