How It Works

01. Image Acquisition

The system captures high-frame-rate video streams to analyze blood flow information via skin color changes.

02. Landmark Detection

Our proprietary neural network maps 468 facial landmarks to detect micro-tensions in muscle groups.

03. Signal Extraction

Remote Photoplethysmography (rPPG) extracts cardiac pulse signals from the facial region of interest.

04. AI Synthesis

All data points are synthesized using a transformer model to output a calibrated stress score.
