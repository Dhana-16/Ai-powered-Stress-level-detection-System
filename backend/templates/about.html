Our Mission

At NeuroCalm, we believe that mental health is the new frontier of human optimization. Our mission is to democratize access to clinical-grade stress analysis using the power of Artificial Intelligence.

We bridge the gap between subjective feelings and objective data, empowering individuals to understand their minds with the same precision they track their fitness.

The Technology

Our proprietary neural engine, "Cortex-V", processes over 50 biometric markers per millisecond. By combining Computer Vision with Remote Photoplethysmography (rPPG), we detect the invisible physiological signals of stress.

We don't just guess; we calculate. Every pixel, every pulse, and every micro-expression is analyzed in real-time.
